About:
Imagine landed/crashed/get lost in the desert, thirsty with no water.
Assuming you could tell your approximate location and outfit, this drone will wind you and drop you a bottle of water.

How cool is that?
Btw, the water would be cooled on delivery ;)
What started as class project (code typing excercise) is evolving into a fully featured fundraising site for a real project.
Kenn's research leads to UAVs flying over (Australian) desert dropping bottled water on a target. This will simulate life support, delivering water to a person out there. And you can support the development and construction of those UAVs.
The $3,000 is a total placeholder and also first estimate of material costs (cameras, aviation electronics, plane parts). We'll publish detailed bill of material as the fundraising accelerates.
There are no tiers of support defined right now. No items chosen to be given away to supporters above certain tresholds. Just go to 'Donate bitcoins' pick a value or enter your own and send us a token of your appreciation. Or retweet..

FAQ

What and when will be bought?
What: Multiple UAVs carying a camera, electronics and a bottle of water - with bottle release mechanisn. Each vehicle will need a camera and whole set of aviation electronics..
When: All I know at the moment is "October".
The plane in the pictures is the current prototype (making aerial pictures, testing antennas, gathering raw data). Firmware is TauLabs fork of openpilot
Tweets and backlinks are welcome. Please use http://9to5passion.eu which is associated in the leaderboard scoring system and is the main landing site.
The fundraising practically started and will be going on until the UAV competition. In the spirit of a short 'kickstarter' campaign we will most probably relaunch avery few two weeks to reset the cointers and keep it interesting aw we will approach milestones. We would like to compare periods of funding to each other, reaching smaller goals one at a time. P.ex. crowdsourcing 1 x digital camera for improved prototype (instead of 3 x camera + 3 x boards & electronics & 3 x plane kits in one big bang we'll focus each round to a specific component).
What exactly will the plane do?
It will take off, fly over the desert and search for a human (adult) sized figurine dressed in a yellow shirt and blue trousers. Once the target is identified & localized on a map, it will drop the payload (bottle of water) as close to it as possible. The bottle must land within 100 meters of the target, preferably closer and contain 500 ml of life saving liquid). The plane must return to the starting point.
Delivery by plane is more effective then a quadcopter (or alike) thus the vehicle choice.
Next experiments involve parachute tests for the water dropping.
