About:
At the moment this is only about the class project. My goal is to keep working on it continuously until October 2013 and help Kenneth fundraise for THE real project. His UAVs will be flying over Australian desert dropping bottled water on a target (simulating life support). Please visit postpone your donations. Current site is more of a working prototype rather then a fully featured fundraising website. We'll be fundraising for UAV elecronics, plane parts and cameras. The $3,000 is first estimate of the costs of cameras and electronics for the competition planes and will be updated when the fundraising really starts (p.ex. bill of material).

FAQ

What and when needs to be build?
What: Multiple copies of an UAV carying a camera and a bottle of water - with bottle release mechanisn (multiple sets of electronics and cameras).
When: All I know is "October"
What exactly will the plane do?
It will be looking for a man sized figurine wearing yellow shirt and blue trousers (in the desert) and once identified, it will drop the bottle of water as close to it as possible (water must land within 100 meters of the target, preferably closer).
