.the challenge
To take off, fly over the specified area and search for a human (adult) sized figurine (dressed in a yellow shirt and blue trousers). Once the target is identified, try to hit it with the bottle of water, note drop zone location and return to the starting base.
The bottle must land within 100 meters of the target and contain 500 ml of life saving liquid. The plane must return to the place where it took off. The bottle should not break on landing.
This is the challenge.
