About:
At the moment this is only about the class project. My goal is to keep working on it continuously until October 2013 and help Kenneth fundraise for THE real project. His UAVs will be flying over Australian desert dropping bottled water on a target (simulating life support). Please visit this site later and postpone your donations to late September 2013 or later. Current site is a working prototype for the course class rather then a fully featured fundraising website. We'll be fundraising for UAV elecronics, plane parts and cameras. The $3,000 is a preliminary estimate of the costs of electronics for the competition planes and will be updated when the fundraising really starts.
