.the challenge
To take off, fly over the desert and search for a human (adult) sized figurine (dressed in a yellow shirt and blue trousers). Once the target is identified, try to hit it with the bottle of water, remember location of the drop zone.
The bottle must land within 100 meters of the target, preferably closer and contain 500 ml of life saving liquid. The plane must return to the starting point.

The other challenge (short-term) is the course leaderboard where I entered this project. CS184 team measures the social and financial feedback. That could explain all the donate/tweet socio-economical buttons.
